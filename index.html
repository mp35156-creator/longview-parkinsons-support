About Parkinson’s Disease

Parkinson’s disease is a progressive neurological condition that affects movement, balance, and coordination. Symptoms can include tremors, stiffness, slowness of movement, and changes in speech or writing.

While there is currently no cure, many treatments and therapies can help manage symptoms and improve quality of life. Ongoing research continues to advance understanding and treatment options.
